# Sanitized Rule Examples

## Noise Notifications

Routine app alerts, social updates, newsletters, and generic status messages are archived unless they contain a true payment failure, deadline, suspension, or direct human ask.

## Key People

Direct human emails from whitelisted relationship contacts are surfaced. Broadcasts, launch emails, and newsletter-style messages from the same people are archived.

## Company Finance

Company invoices, merchant account issues, vendor billing, and payment processor problems are routed to the finance delegate.

## Personal Admin Finance

Household, vehicle, personal subscription, personal insurance, and personal finance items are routed to the personal admin.

## Domain Expiration Risk

Registrar expiration and payment failure notices are surfaced urgently to the executive.
