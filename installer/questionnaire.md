# Install Questionnaire

Use this questionnaire to configure a new installation.

## Source Inboxes

- What Gmail or Google Workspace accounts should be scanned?
- What short prefix should identify each inbox?
- Which inbox is primary?
- Should the first run look back 14 days, 30 days, or another window?

## Important People

- Who should always be considered important if they send a direct personal email?
- Which people are important only for specific topics?
- Which senders look important but often send newsletters or promotional broadcasts?

## Delegates

- Who handles company finance?
- Who handles personal or household finance?
- Who handles operations?
- Who handles legal/compliance?
- Who handles technical incidents?
- Who should be CC'd on delegated items?

## Executive Visibility

- What should always reach the executive?
- What should never reach the executive?
- What should go into a digest?
- What should send an urgent notification?

## Notifications

- Should urgent items go to chat, text, WhatsApp, or another channel?
- Should every urgent text also be copied to a chat reply channel?
- What hours should automated notification runs be active?

## Safety

- May the agent send emails automatically?
- Should delegation be draft-first?
- May the agent archive clear noise?
- May the agent delete spam?
